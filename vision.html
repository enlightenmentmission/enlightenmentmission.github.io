Our Vision

“Right To Education ” is true now a days for allmost all Indians, but right to better education is only confined to a handful privileged ones. Computer education is one such part that has only been for the privileged ones. But for the synthesis of a golden India, everyone must get equal chances and priveileges. Our mission is to reach each and every unprivileged kid and provide them the platform to feel privileged enough.
“ Freedom is our birth right and we must have it. ” ---- Subhash Chandra Bose. So why leave freedom in the computer world? Computer education in India is based on non-free or proprietary platform which takes our freedom from us. It is astonishing that computer users of Independent India are still not independent and constantly being exploited by proprietary IT industry. Our mission is to provide the platform of freedom to kids so that Indians will be free of such exploits in future. We will use FOSS (Free and Open-Source Software) alongwith OSH (Open-Source Hardware) in this mission. The principles of Free (in sense of Freedom) Software as per FSF are:
  • The freedom to run the program as you wish, for any purpose (freedom 0).
  • The freedom to study how the program works, and change it so it does your computing as you wish (freedom 1). Access to the source code is a precondition for this.
  • The freedom to redistribute copies so you can help your neighbor (freedom 2).
  • The freedom to distribute copies of your modified versions to others (freedom 3). By doing this you can give the whole community a chance to benefit from your changes. Access to the source code is a precondition for this.
In short, “Free software” means software that respects users' freedom and community. Roughly, it means that the users have the freedom to run, copy, distribute, study, change and improve the software. Thus, “free software” is a matter of liberty, not price.
Application of Education in life will make us step further towards Golden India. Application comes out of creativity and passion. We will try our best to provide a platform that awakens the creavity of young minds. We want to make education fun and joy so that one day it becomes passion and outcome immense creativity and innovation.
